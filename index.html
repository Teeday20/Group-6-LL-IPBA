STREAM OUTLINE

The stream outline can help us to organize our thoughts and ideas, making it easier for us to communicate our message effectively to our audience. It keeps us on course and prevents us from straying from the subject. During the stream, an outline can also act as a reference guide for us, making it easier for us to locate the data or the information we need to respond to inquiries. Additionally, it can also help us in determining gaps in our knowledge or areas that require further research prior to the stream. Overall it helps us to stay organized, focused, and on course and it serves as a helpful reference manual for both us and our audience.
